GOLDEN PEARL

Golden Pearl Cosmetics is an independent family owned by Pakistani producer of beauty enhancing & caring products which was established in 1997 by Mr. Sheikh Abid Mehmood (Chairman Golden pearl Cosmetics). Golden Pearl Beauty Cream is the pioneer amongst the whitening beauty creams in Pakistan. Golden pearl has specialized in Beauty Care products by maintaining the winning combination of safety, efficacy, affordability and continuous innovation and is now enjoying leading position in the beauty industry.

at all levels.
